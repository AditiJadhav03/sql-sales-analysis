&nbsp;1. Category Performance



The Technology category generated the highest revenue among all product categories.

This indicates strong customer demand for electronic and technology products.

The business should prioritize stocking and promoting technology products to maximize revenue.







&nbsp;2. Top Selling Products



A small number of products contribute a large portion of the total sales.

These products should always be kept in stock and monitored to avoid stock-out situations.

They can also be used in promotional campaigns to attract customers.







&nbsp;3. Low Performing Products



Some products show very low sales compared to others.

These items may be slow-moving inventory and can increase storage costs.

The company should consider discounting, bundling, or reducing future orders of such products.







&nbsp;4. Customer Purchase Behavior



The analysis identified that a few customers generate significantly higher revenue than others.

This indicates the presence of high-value customers.

The company can implement loyalty programs, special offers, or membership benefits to retain these customers.







5\. Shipping Preference



Most customers selected Standard Class shipping over faster delivery options.

This suggests customers are price-sensitive and prefer lower shipping cost rather than faster delivery.

The company can optimize logistics operations by focusing on efficient standard shipping services.





&nbsp;6. Regional Performance



The West region produced the highest total sales, followed by the East region.

This indicates strong market demand in these areas and potential opportunities for expansion.

The South region showed the lowest performance, suggesting a need for improved marketing strategies and store performance evaluation.







&nbsp;7. Monthly Sales Trend (Seasonality)



Monthly sales show a clear seasonal pattern.

Sales increase significantly from September and peak during November and December, indicating holiday or festive season demand.

February recorded the lowest sales, representing an off-season period.



The company can use this insight to:



\- Increase inventory before peak months

\- Plan marketing campaigns in advance

\- Hire temporary staff during high demand periods

\- Prepare logistics and delivery operations
